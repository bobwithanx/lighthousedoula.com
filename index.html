Sue Marquis, PCD(DONA)

A DONA certified post-partum doula serving the Phoenix area.

Welcome

A new addition to your family means lots of changes! As a postpartum doula, I am here to help you adjust to them. You may want an extra pair of hands as soon as you bring your new baby home, or maybe you'll need them in the weeks or months after.

Postpartum help is also a wonderful gift to give new parents as a shower or baby gift. I offer several options that can be given as a gift. Contact me to purchase a specific amount of hours, a day, or several days of postpartum help.

Experience

I have been working with children for much of the past 40 years. In addition to my own son and my young granddaughter, I spent many years running an independent childcare service from my home. I am now a certified postpartum doula and have been working with families since January 2013.

Certification

I understand the importance of hiring someone you can trust, and certification is an important demonstration of this to the families with whom I work. I am a certified DONA International postpartum doula and have completed Professional Education in Breastfeeding training.

What is a postpartum doula?

A postpartum doula assists moms and families as they adjust and care for their new babies.

Postpartum doulas can assist in the following areas:

  • breastfeeding support & consultation
  • baby care
  • cooking
  • laundry
  • family life adjustments to new baby
